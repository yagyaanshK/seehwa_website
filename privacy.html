Respectingvisitor privacy.

This website currently functions as an informational public-facing site for SEEHWA Green Foundation. Donation, newsletter and form integrations are placeholders until official systems are connected.

This static website does not currently collect payment information, newsletter subscriptions or contact-form submissions directly. Email links open the visitor's own email client.

When newsletter, contact, UPI or payment gateway systems are connected, this policy should be updated to describe what data is collected, why it is collected, how long it is retained and which third-party processors are used.
