About Us

Science-backedaction forLadakh.

SEEHWA Green Foundation is a Section 8 non-profit company headquartered in Ladakh, dedicated to advancing the Sustainable Development Goals through practical action in one of the world's most fragile high-altitude ecosystems.

Vision

Resilient, net-zero, circular mountain communities.

We envision high-altitude regions of Ladakh and beyond thriving in harmony with nature, guided by local wisdom and strengthened by modern science.

Our mission is to lead transformative change across cold and fragile ecosystems by designing net-zero energy systems, embedding circular economy principles, delivering research-driven consulting, building local capacity and fusing traditional knowledge with modern innovation.

Recognition

Recipient of the 2026 NaropaCare Grant.

NaropaCare supports locally rooted organizations addressing healthcare access challenges in Ladakh. SEEHWA Green Foundation has been recognized in the 2026 grant cycle, strengthening our work in community health, preventive care and last-mile access.
